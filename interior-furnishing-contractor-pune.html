How we work

From Briefing
to Handover

Our interior furnishing process is built around your construction timeline — transparent, milestone-based, and designed for zero-surprise delivery on large residential and commercial projects.

01
Site Brief & BOQ

We visit your site, understand scope — kitchens, wardrobes, carpentry, polishing — and prepare a detailed BOQ with transparent pricing per unit.

02
Design & Sample Approval

We prepare design options and physical samples — materials, finishes, hardware — for your approval before any bulk work begins.

03
Manufacturing & Execution

Furniture and carpentry items manufactured at our Hadapsar facility. Site teams begin on-site fit-out as per floor-wise schedule.

04
Finishing & Handover

Polishing, painting, final hardware fitting — unit by unit. Quality check before every handover. Snagging resolved on-site immediately.
