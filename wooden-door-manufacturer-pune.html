How we work

From Enquiry
to Installation

Our supply process is designed for builders and project managers — fast, transparent, and built around your site schedule. No delays, no surprises. Just doors delivered and installed on time.

01
Site Visit & BOQ

We visit your site, assess door count, sizes, and specifications, and prepare a detailed Bill of Quantities with transparent pricing.

02
Sample & Approval

We provide door samples for your approval — material, finish, and hardware — before bulk manufacturing begins. No guesswork.

03
Manufacturing

Production at our Hadapsar facility or on-site depending on project scale. All materials sourced from approved suppliers only.

04
Delivery & Installation

Tower-wise, floor-wise delivery as per your construction schedule. Our teams handle complete installation and finishing.
