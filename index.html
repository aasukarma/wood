Our Story

Rooted in
Tradition
Built for Tomorrow

Motion Woods was born from a deep-rooted legacy of woodworking that spans generations. Our founder, Diwakar Vishwakarma, grew up in a small village in Uttar Pradesh watching his father transform raw wood into art — and brought that vision to the cities of Maharashtra.

Today, our work reflects a unique blend of innovation, aesthetics, and excellence. Each creation embodies the soul of authenticity, the grace of pure handcraftsmanship, and the grandeur of modern design — serving some of Pune's most prestigious residential and commercial developers.

Diwakar Vishwakarma
Founder & Master Craftsman

From working alongside renowned architects in Mumbai to leading large-scale residential projects across Pune, Diwakar's journey is one of relentless dedication to craft. His hands-on approach ensures every project meets the Motion Woods standard of excellence.

"Every product, every creation is a journey — inspired by tradition, enriched by experience."
